Glossary:

  • State: The numbers the model sees right now.
  • Action: One choice the model can make (turn, brake, accelerate).
  • Reward: Score signal after an action. Positive means “more like this.”
  • Episode: One full attempt until crash or max steps.
  • Policy: The model’s strategy for choosing actions.
  • Inference: Running the trained model to pick an action.
